Joseph Joseph CleanTech CT3 Replacement Pad

Upgrade your cleaning game with the Joseph Joseph CleanTech CT3 Replacement Pad. Designed for maximum versatility, this pad combines a soft microfiber side with a powerful abrasive side to tackle every cleaning challenge with ease.

Whether you're refreshing your windows, restoring your bathroom surfaces, or deep cleaning kitchen tiles, the CT3 pad is your go-to solution. The microfiber side is gentle and safe for glass, glossy tiles, bathtubs, and shower trays, preventing scratches and preserving the finish. On the other hand, the abrasive side is specifically engineered to remove stubborn stains and limescale efficiently, saving you time and effort.

With its machine-washable construction, the Joseph Joseph replacement pad is reusable and eco-friendly. This means you can keep it in your cleaning routine for long-term value, reducing waste and cleaning costs. Its easy-to-fit design makes replacing the pad a quick and hassle-free process, ensuring a secure grip and consistent performance.

Perfectly compatible with the Joseph Joseph CleanTech 2-in-1 Surface Scrubber, this pad offers a practical and effective way to streamline your cleaning tasks. It's a small but essential upgrade that makes a big difference in your daily routine.
